Hello,
my name is Anna.

I am an aspiring graphic designer based in Chicago, with a love for typography and the color purple.


I grew up in the Metro Detroit area, and spent most of my time drawing or daydreaming [with the exception of playing video games every now and then]. I am currently studying graphic design at DePaul University, and plan to pursue a minor in marketing.


This portfolio is an ongoing work in progress that contains an updated selection of my design work. I aim to create accessible and visually appealing designs that are effective in communicating information, and of course, spreading positivity.


Feel free to take a look around!
